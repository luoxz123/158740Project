# Demo Flow

1. Open the frontend map.
2. Show local GeoJSON layers for wind, solar, transmission, protected areas, and GIR points.
3. Filter to solar zones with score above 80.
4. Search for `Marlborough` and open the popup chart.
5. Enable the heatmap layer.
6. Switch on GeoServer WMS after confirming GeoServer is running.
7. Show dashboard chart changes when filters change.
8. Explain the GIR workflow and show generated renewable energy mention points.
9. Open PostGIS queries to demonstrate CRS validation and spatial filtering.
